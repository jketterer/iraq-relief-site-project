How to Help

Consider Donating

Check out some of our most trusted organizations that offer aid and services to the victims of the Iraq Civil War.

Doctors Without Borders

Doctors Without Borders is a France-based organization that provides the displaced people of Iraq with urgent medical care.

Visit

World Vision

World Vision works to provide displaced Iraqis with food, water, sanitation, shelter, and saftey.

Visit

Rescue.org

This organization provides displaced residents of Iraq with water and hygiene resources, as well as works in Sryian refugee camps to offer education and legal services to women and children.

Visit

Preemptive Love

This organization provides "clothing, housing, education, and legal help for Iraqi children and their famileies suffering from persecution and desplacement."

Visit

Save the Children

This organization specializes in sending supplies to children and families affected by the violence in Iraq.
