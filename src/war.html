Overview

Beginnings

The war began in January 2014 when the militant group ISIS infiltrated and captured the cities of Fallujah and Ramadi after months of escalating violence.

With the ultimate goal of capturing Iraq's capital city, Baghdad, ISIS continued to capture territory. Throughout the rest of 2014, Kurdish and US forces assist the Iraqi government in repelling ISIS attacks on various cities.

ISIS managed to capture the cities of Mosul and Tikrit before the Iraqi government was able to launch a full assault against the guerilla troops.
